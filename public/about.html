About the Creator

Hi! I'm Nikki, a Computer Science graduate with a passion for both technology and creative design. I created Gold Designer Nails as a way to combine my love for coding with my love for nail art. Every nail set you see here is designed by me, and the entire website was built from scratch using JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, jQuery, and more.

How It Started

As someone who loves stylish nails but knows how time-consuming and expensive salon visits can be, I decided to design my own press-on nails. While designing nails quickly became a creative outlet, I also wanted to challenge myself as a developer by building an e-commerce site from the ground up.
